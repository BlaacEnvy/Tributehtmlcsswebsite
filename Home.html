Priscilla Shirer

I remember watching war room as teenager and that changed my life. At that age prayer was more of a ritual than a dialogue with God. But after watching war room something sparked in me, and I couldn't get this phenomenal woman out of my mind. With a few google searches I found out that her name was Priscilla Shirer.
I began following on social media , listening to her teaching and the more i did this the more inspired I got. By not only her preaching but also her values, virtues and her firm faith in Jesus Christ. She is truly a diamond in a raft in generation where young girl need upright role models.
